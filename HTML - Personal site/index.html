The Ed Annan phenomenon

Founder and CEO of the stay-at-home father brigade

Fathers in the modern age are facing uncertainty in these ever-changing times of personal, social and economic change.
My aim is to bring some direction to fathers so they can redefine themselves for the 21st century.
Don't be afraid to stay at home, don't be afraid to take on a different role, and, above all, don't be afraid to embrace the joy of caring for your little ones.
Be there for them; be there for yourself!
